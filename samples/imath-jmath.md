# Bold italic math letters

Ordinary italic variables appear first. The requested dotted bold-italic
command glyphs follow, including accented forms.

Inline: $i, j, \imath, \jmath, \hat{\imath}, \hat{\jmath}$.

Display:

$$
i + j + \imath + \jmath
$$

$$
\hat{\imath} + \vec{\jmath} + \imath_1^2 + \jmath_k^n
$$
